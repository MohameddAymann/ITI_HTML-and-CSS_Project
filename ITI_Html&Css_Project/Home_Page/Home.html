About ITI

The Information Technology Institute ( ITI ) was established back in 1993 and became one of the MCIT's affiliates in 2005. Its main mission revolves around ICT human capacity development. For over 28 years, ITI has kept updating its training portfolio through continuous monitoring of future global tech trends, as well as the country's national and mega projects. As a result, it sustained an 82 % employment percentage for its graduates.
